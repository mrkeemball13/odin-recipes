Kuey Teow Recipes

Description:

Ingredients

  • Flat rice noodles (kuey teow)
  • Protein of choice (chicken, prawns, beef, or tofu)
  • Vegetables (bean sprouts, Chinese chives, or bok choy)
  • Soy sauce (light and dark)
  • Oyster sauce
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Eggs
  1. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add protein and cook until done.
  3. Push the protein aside, scramble eggs, and mix together.
  4. Add kuey teow noodles and season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a bit of sugar.
  5. Toss in vegetables and stir-fry briefly until well combined.
