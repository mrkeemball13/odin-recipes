Roti Canai Recipes

Description:

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Kosher salt
  • Granulated sugar
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Beaten egg
  • Whole milk
  1. Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl. Add ghee and mix until crumbly.
  2. Add egg and milk to form a dough, then knead until smooth.
  3. Rest the dough for a few hours or overnight.
  4. Divide into balls, flatten and stretch into thin sheets.
  5. Fry on a hot griddle with a little ghee until golden and crispy.
